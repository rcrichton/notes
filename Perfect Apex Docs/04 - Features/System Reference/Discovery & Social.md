Features that turn telemetry into a shared experience.

## Community Hub
*   **Public Session Feed**:
    *   **Explore**: Filter public sessions by Make, Model, and Track.
    *   **Recently Added**: See the latest community uploads.
*   **Leaderboards**:
    *   Track-specific leaderboards filterable by Layout.
    *   Rankings based on Best Lap time.

## Personalized Recommendations
*   **"Because You Drove"**: Suggestions based on your vehicle history.
*   **"Improve Your Time"**: Faster laps from others on tracks you've driven.

## Comparison & Sharing
*   **Session Comparison**: Compare your session against any public session or your own history. Side-by-side metric analysis.
*   **Share Links**: Generate public links for sessions to share with friends or coaches.

## Social Story Integration
Create viral-ready assets customized for Instagram Stories, TikTok, or Snapchat.
*   **High-Res Generation**: Auto-generates 1080x1920px (9:16) PNG images.
*   **Dynamic Themes**: Choose from multiple styles like "Clean Day", "Carbon", or user-exclusive "Neon Night".
*   **Data Models**:
    *   **Session Stats**: Highlights consistency, total laps, and top 5 lap times.
    *   **PB Mode**: Showcases the track map, best lap time, and peak G-forces.
*   **Smart Linking**: Embedded QR Code directs viewers straight to the interactive session data.
