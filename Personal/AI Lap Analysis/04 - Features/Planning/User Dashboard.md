## Profile Identity
*   **Profile Picture**: User avatar or uploaded photo.
*   **Bio**: Short description or driver profile.
*   **Stats Overview**: High-level summary of total laps, tracks driven, and active days.

## Performance Tracking
*   **Latest Sessions**: Quick access to recent uploads.
*   **Fastest Laps**: Automatic aggregation of best times per track.
*   **Driving Style Insights**: (Future) Long-term analysis of driving habits (e.g., "Late Braker", "Smooth Steerer").

## User Garage
*   **Summary**: Quick list of vehicles driven.
*   **Management**: Detailed vehicle and modification tracking is handled in the [[User Garage]].
