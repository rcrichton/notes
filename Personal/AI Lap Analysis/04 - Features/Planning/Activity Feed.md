# Activity Feed & Social Graph

## Core Features (Planned)
*   **Activity Feed**: A central stream showing:
    *   New session uploads from followed users.
    *   "Likes" on sessions or specific laps.
    *   Achievement unlocks (e.g., "New Personal Best").
    *   *Monetization Potential*: Sponsored posts for new devices or performance parts.
*   **Follow System**:
    *   Follow drivers to see their activity.
    *   "Follower Activity" digest.
*   **Notification Center**:
    *   New chat messages in shared sessions.
    *   "What's New" platform updates.
    *   Activity from followed users.
