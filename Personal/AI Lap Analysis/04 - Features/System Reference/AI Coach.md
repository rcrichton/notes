Proactive, automated analysis powered by Google Gemini Pro.

## Automated Insights
*   **Session Summary**: AI generates a conversational summary of performance immediately post-upload.
*   **Structured Opportunities**: Identifies specific "Key Opportunities" (e.g., "Brake later into T1") and improving tags.

## Conversational Assistant
*   **Chat Interface**: Ask the AI questions about your data ("Where am I losing time vs my best lap?").
*   **Context Aware**: The AI has access to lap stats, metadata, and telemetry samples.
