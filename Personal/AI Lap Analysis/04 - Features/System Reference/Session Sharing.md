
Features that facilitate collaboration and competitive benchmarking.

## Sharing Mechanics
*   **Public Links**: Generate secure, unique URLs to share specific sessions with anyone, even without an account.
*   **Visibility Controls**:
    *   **Private**: Visible only to the owner.
    *   **Shared**: Visible to anyone with the link (Unlisted).
    *   **Public**: Discoverable by the community and indexed in leaderboards.

## Session Comparison
A powerful tool to benchmark performance against other drivers or your own past sessions.

*   **Discovery**: Find sessions to compare against via:
    *   **My History**: Previous sessions at the same track.
    *   **Community**: Public sessions from other drivers.
    *   **Leaderboards**: Top laps for the specific track layout.
*   **Analysis View**:
    *   **reference Lap**: Set a specific lap from the comparison session as the "Reference".
    *   **Delta Calculation**: Automatic calculation of time delta between your lap and the reference.
    *   **Ghost Overlay**: Visual comparison of racing lines on the track map.
